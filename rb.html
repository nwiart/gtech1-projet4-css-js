Roll-A-Ball!

Roll-A-Ball! is a 3D platformer game driven by one crucial gameplay rule : you control a ball with your mouse. The faster you move your mouse, the faster the ball will roll.

The game is divided into many levels, each with a finish zone. The only goal is to complete all levels as quickly as possible.

The first version of Roll-A-Ball! Alpha was released in 2019. The game included basic levels made out of platforms, custom levels, challenges, etc... Beta's major change was supposed to be the addition of a multiplayer mode, but the development of a correct networking API struggled for a long time. The last Beta version has not made it past the "work-in-progress" state and was originally scheduled for July 2020. Past this date, development was abandoned.
