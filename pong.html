History

Here is pong, a remake of the world's first arcade game, created by Allan Alcorn in 1972. This game revolutionized the era of video games. To find out how and what it felt Allan Alcorn during its development, my teammate and mi decided to recreate this famous game, adding our personal touch of course.

Development phases
